# Game Design

## Themes
Scavenging
Always a bigger fish

## Goal
Descend down 5 levels and get the mcguffin

## Progression
Progression through items instead of through skills or abilities

## Items
* Sword
* Axe
* Club

* Leather Armor (0 speed penalty)
* Chainmail (-25 speed penalty)
* Platemail (-50 speed penalty)

* Shortbow
* Longbow
* Heavy bow

* Rock
